Professional Pre-Incident Planning

Alliance Disaster Kleenup provides crucial planning tools and service agreements in our Priority Response Emergency Planning (P.R.E.P) program. The importance of being proactive vs. reactive in terms of disaster recovery planning has never been greater. With Alliance Disaster Kleenup, your business can be back up and running shortly after even the most damaging of disasters.

Disaster planning offers you the opportunity to make decisions and commit those decisions to action items in the quiet of your office prior to a disaster taking place. ​Plans vary widely from one organization to another, but an Alliance Disaster Kleenup representative will meet with you to customize a plan that fits your company’s needs best.

​Priority Response Emergency Planning (P.R.E.P) Benefits

Proactively planning for disaster restoration mitigates risks, expedites response times, and minimizes downtime. It ensures swift and efficient recovery, safeguarding assets, and reducing the impact of unforeseen events on business operations. When you work with us, some of the benefits include:

Highest Priority

Your business will be given a priority response number that guarantees immediate response after regional catastrophes that often create back log and increased waiting times.

Better Prices

In some circumstances, reduced pricing can be implemented to save your business costs of equipment used in response to your disaster.
